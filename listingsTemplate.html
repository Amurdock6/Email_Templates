This magnificent residence rests at the end of a private cul-de-sac, 6,200 feet above sea level, on Draper’s east bench crystal-clear views of the entire Salt Lake valley and surrounding mountains make this a standout home among other luxury properties in Utah. Framed by a massive picture window in the main living area with 23′ vaulted ceilings, views orient towards the northwest and include the great Salt Lake, glimpses of Idaho mountain ranges to the north as well as downtown Salt Lake City. Designed and built in 2004, this custom Parade of Homes masterpiece is extremely well built and replete with amenities perfect for the post-Covid era. Features include an open main level floorplan which includes a gourmet kitchen and dining area plus a gracious family room with a massive floor to ceiling rock fireplace. The inside space connects to the outdoors via a 1,500 square foot wraparound deck constructed of South African hardwood. The main level primary suite boasts an oversized, en-suite bathroom with an oversized shower and dressing rooms plus en-suite laundry. Two home offices, connected via a bridgeway, are easily accessed from the main level. Views abound in this home, including from the lower level, which features a large living area with 10′ ceilings, kitchenette, two bedrooms each with en-suite bathrooms, a 10 seat theater room plus a must-see ‘panic’ room, complete with a huge Fort Knox fire-rated door, dedicated phone line, and wired for a CCTV system. Other features throughout the home include radiant heat, built in Sonos speakers & amps, a wet-dry vac system plus a commercial grade Luxel wi-fi system. To complete this masterpiece, the owners designed a huge garage that can easily accommodate 4 cars or 2 cars + an RV (accessible via a 12 ft. copper door). Additional garage features include an electric vehicle (Tesla) charging station plus plenty of storage. The garage is exceptional with its brand-new epoxy flooring, workstation and 15′ ceilings. Located in the heart of Utah’s burgeoning tech corridor, the home’s location is inevitably in one of the “best bike-in-bike” out locations in the Salt Lake Valley. It’s just a short ride to a trailhead which ties into the Salt Lake Valley’s greatest biking and hiking trails systems, including the Corner Canyon trails, all totaling 117 acres of scenic trials located within 5,000 acres of open space. 1459 E Meadow Bluff Lane is a perfect family home, “work from home” home or ski home.
